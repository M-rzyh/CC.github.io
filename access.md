# 1. Getting Access

## Step 1 — Create an Account
Go to:
https://ccdb.computecanada.ca/account_application

You will need:
- University institutional email
- Supervisor/PI name
- Research project description

Your PI must approve the request.

---

## Step 2 — Link Your Institutional Identity
Use the "Institutional Login" method (EduID Canada).

---

## Step 3 — Set Up MFA (University-specific)
Most institutions require Duo/Google Authenticator.

---

## Step 4 — Wait for Approval
PI approval + system processing usually takes **24–72 hours**.

---

## Step 5 — Choose a Cluster
You may be assigned access to:
- Cedar
- Graham
- Béluga
- Narval
- Niagara

You don’t need to choose manually.
